065 Alakazam

Previous Next

Psychic

Height: 1.5 m (4'11")

Weight: 48.0 kg (105.8 lbs)

Abilities: Synchronize, Inner Focus

Alakazam wields potent psychic powers. It's said that this Pokémon used these powers to create the spoons it holds. It has an incredibly high level of intelligence and an IQ of approximately 5,000.
