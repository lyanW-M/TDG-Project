143 Snorlax

Previous Next

Normal

Sleeping

Height: 2.1 m (6'11")

Weight: 460.0 kg (1014.1 lbs)

Abilities: Immunity

Snorlax is a massive Pokémon that spends most of its time sleeping. It is known for its immense strength and its voracious appetite when it does wake up. Snorlax often blocks roads, forcing trainers to figure out how to wake it up.
