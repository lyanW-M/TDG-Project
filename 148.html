Dragon

Height: 4.0 m (13'01")

Weight: 16.5 kg (36.4 lbs)

Abilities: Shed Skin

Dragonair is a mystical Dragon-type Pokémon with a graceful, serpentine body. It is known for its elegant nature and is often seen as a symbol of good fortune. Dragonair can control the weather and is said to evolve into the mighty Dragonite.
