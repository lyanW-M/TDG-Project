019 Rattata

Normal

Height: 0.3 m (1'00")

Weight: 3.5 kg (7.7 lbs)

Abilities: Run Away, Guts (hidden ability)

Ratatta is cautious in the extreme. Even while it is asleep, it constantly listens by moving its ears around. It is not picky in where it lives - it will make its nest everywhere.
