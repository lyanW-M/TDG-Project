Psychic

Height: 2.0 m (6'07")

Weight: 122.0 kg (269.0 lbs)

Mewtwo is a Psychic-type Pokémon that was created through genetic manipulation of the DNA of Mew. It is one of the most powerful Pokémon in existence, possessing incredible psychic abilities. Mewtwo was designed to be a perfect weapon, but it seeks its own identity and purpose.
