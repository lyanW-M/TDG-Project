113 Chansey

Previous Next

Normal

Height: 1.1 m (3'07")

Weight: 34.6 kg (76.3 lbs)

Abilities: Natural Cure, Serene Grace

Chansey is a gentle Pokémon that brings happiness to people. It is said to heal injuries with the eggs it carries and is often found in hospitals or near people in need.
