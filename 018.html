018 Pidgeot

Normal

Flying

Height: 1.5 m (4'11")

Weight: 39.5 kg (87.1 lbs)

Abilities: Keen Eye, Tangled Feet (hidden ability)

Pidgeot is known for its speed and agility. It can fly at incredible speeds, and its wings can generate gusts of wind strong enough to blow away enemies. In Pokémon GO, it's one of the top-tier Flying-type attackers.
