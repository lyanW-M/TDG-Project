Flying

Height: 2.2 m (7'03")

Dragonite is the final form of the Dragonair evolutionary line. It is a strong and friendly Dragon/Flying-type Pokémon. Known for its incredible speed and power, Dragonite is often considered a champion among Dragon-types.
