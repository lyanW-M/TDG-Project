Dragon

Height: 1.8 m (5'11")

Weight: 3.3 kg (7.3 lbs)

Dratini is a Dragon-type Pokémon that is known for its serpentine body and its potential to evolve into a powerful Dragonite. Dratini is often found in bodies of water and is a rare and elusive species.
