055 Golduck

Previous Next

Water

Height: 1.7 m (5'07")

Weight: 76.6 kg (168.9 lbs)

Abilities: Damp, Cloud Nine

Golduck is a blue, bipedal, duck, or platypus-like Pokémon. It has red eyes, a cream-colored beak, a red gem in the center of its forehead, and four spikes on the back of its head. Its hands and feet all have three clawed digits with cream-colored webbing in between. Its body is specially adapted to have a hydrodynamic shape, and it has a long tail that it uses as a rudder to steer.
